Privacy Policy

App: BIO

Last updated: May 10, 2026

BIO is a well-being and focus app designed to provide audio experiences, mindfulness content, and user-controlled digital wellness features.

1. Information We Collect

BIO does not require users to create an account to access the basic app experience.

The app may process information locally on the user's device to provide app functionality, including preferences, selected content, subscription status, focus settings, and user-authorized Screen Time or Family Controls interactions when applicable.

2. Purchases and Subscriptions

BIO offers optional premium subscriptions through Apple's In-App Purchase system. Purchases, billing, renewals, cancellations, refunds, and payment information are handled by Apple. BIO does not collect or store users' credit card or payment details.

3. Family Controls and Screen Time Features

If the user enables features based on Apple's Family Controls, DeviceActivity, ManagedSettings, or related Screen Time APIs, BIO uses those permissions only to provide user-authorized focus, restriction, or device wellness functionality.

BIO does not use these features for hidden monitoring, unauthorized surveillance, or control without user consent.

4. Analytics and Diagnostics

BIO may receive limited diagnostic or performance information provided by Apple or the operating system, such as crash reports or usage diagnostics, when users have enabled sharing of such data with developers through Apple settings.

5. Data Sharing

BIO does not sell personal data. BIO does not share personal data with advertisers or data brokers.

6. Data Retention

Information stored locally on the device remains on the user's device unless removed by the user, reset through app settings, or deleted when the app is uninstalled.

7. Children's Privacy

BIO is intended for general well-being, focus, and digital wellness use. When Family Controls or Screen Time permissions are used, they are handled through Apple's authorization mechanisms and user-controlled system permissions.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
